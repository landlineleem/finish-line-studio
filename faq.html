Answers to the questions designers ask before booking their first install day with us.

Frequently asked questions — click to expand

Finish Line Studio currently serves Charlotte (NC), Austin (TX), Nashville (TN), and New York City. Each city has a dedicated team member — Sarah in Austin, Calista in Nashville, and Delaney in NYC. Charlotte installs are handled by the founding team. We're always open to discussing travel for large or multi-day projects.

Booking is simple — visit our Schedule page, choose the team member in your city, and select a date through their individual Acuity booking page. You'll receive an immediate confirmation with everything you need. For larger projects or custom requirements, use the contact form on the same page and we'll be in touch within one business day.

We arrive at the project site ready to work — coffee in hand, tools at the ready. The day typically starts with rug rolling and heavy placement, moves through artwork hanging and drapery steaming, and finishes with styling details: cushions, florals, cable management, and a final sweep. We take direction from you throughout and work at your pace. The goal is always the same: you walk away from the reveal proud of every corner.

Our core install day services include rug rolling and placement, artwork hanging (gallery walls and single statement pieces), drapery steaming, cushion and pillow fluffing, bedding steaming, floral arrangements, cable management and cord concealment, sticker and packaging removal, box breakdown and disposal, floor sweeping, and day-of errands. We also provide warehouse team oversight for deliveries and larger logistics coordination. See our full Services page for descriptions of each.

We recommend booking 2–3 weeks in advance for standard install days, and 4–6 weeks for large multi-room projects or installs in high-demand months (spring and fall are our busiest). That said, we understand design timelines can shift — reach out even if your date is close and we'll do our best to accommodate. Last-minute availability sometimes opens up.

We bring our own professional tools — steamers, hanging hardware, levels, picture rail hooks — so you don't need to source those. For specialty items like furniture felt pads, specific hardware, or floral supplies, we can either source them on your behalf as part of errands or you can supply them. We'll clarify requirements when you book so there are no surprises on install day.

Rates are project-based and vary by city, scope, and duration. We don't publish a price list because every install day is different — a one-room styling appointment is a different commitment than a full multi-day reveal. When you book through Acuity, you'll see the day rate for your city. For custom quotes on larger or recurring projects, use the contact form and we'll respond with a detailed proposal.

Book each day individually through Acuity, or contact us directly to discuss a multi-day package. Designers who work with us regularly often retain a standing weekly or monthly slot.
